CURRENT OPENINGS AT FIREBRAND

Machine Learning Engineer

Location: Seattle, WA | Los Angeles, CA | New York, NY | SF Bay Area | Remote

About the Job


Firebrand is a pureplay analytics consultancy that delivers client impact via digital analytics, data platforms, and artificial intelligence solutions. Founded by analytics industry veterans, Firebrand values innovation, integrity, and impact. We are at the forefront of some of the largest AI transformations in the world. We pride ourselves on designing, building, and implementing innovative solutions which drive real value for our clients, helping them improve operational efficiencies, improve customer satisfaction, and create new revenue streams. As a new company, we are entrepreneurial and always looking to shape our business to meet the rapidly changing requirements of our customers.

The Machine Learning Engineer works with data scientists and proprietary & industry tools to create insights and solutions in line with customer strategies. The successful candidate enjoys diving deeply into problems, can break complex problems into addressable chunks, and communicates well (both written and verbal).

The successful candidate will have experience with big data, advanced statistics, analytics engineering languages (e.g. SQL, Python), and one or more cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, or Google.) This person will understand how to interpret requirements, ask probing questions, and design & build analytics solutions and services that enable machine learning techniques to deliver results in high-scale environments. The role will understand experimentation and develop hypotheses around key strategies and insights, and excel at managing ambiguity and competing priorities.

The ideal candidate is highly collaborative, but self-driven with a strong bias for action and an ability to understand and deliver ambiguous projects with incomplete data. They are a strong technical thinker focused on business intelligence with expertise in machine learning models, modeling environments, and data cleansing & enrichment. This role requires a resourceful mindset that finds creative solutions to complex technical challenges.


Job Responsibilities


  • Develop analytics solutions leveraging big data technologies to ingest, process and analyze large, disparate data sets to meet/exceed business requirements
  • Unify, enrich, and analyze customer engagement and telemetry data to derive insights and opportunities.
  • Clearly communicate findings, recommendations, and opportunities to improve data systems and solutions.
  • Ensure thorough and complete delivery of all committed functionality in a manner that meets or exceeds client expectations.
  • Gather and analyze statistical data using modern and traditional methods to formulate reports and make recommendations.
  • Assist in pioneering new approaches and work to best frame the use of data in all use cases from understanding churn and helping convert high value customers.
  • Devise and evaluate methods for collecting customer data.
  • Integrate ML models with upstream and downstream services and applications.
  • Write scripts for insight generation automation (i.e., customer profile generation).
  • Collaborate with client teams to assess performance data and bring optimization solutions and recommendations.
  • Keep the client and Firebrand managers apprised of current work items and completion dates.

Required Qualifications


  • 2+ years hands on with advanced analytics projects on big data platforms.
  • Experience working in or with large enterprises and large data sets.
  • Experience articulating and translating business questions and using statistical techniques to arrive at an answer using available data.
  • Experience within a managed ML modeling environment.
  • Strong analytical skills and ability to quickly transform the data into presentable formats.
  • Ability to travel up to 25%.
  • Ability to work independently and set priorities for self and others.
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, AI/ML, or Mathematics.

Preferred Qualifications


  • Experience designing and building ML modeling environments.
  • Experience building web service APIs to support data analytics algorithms.
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Prior consulting experience.
