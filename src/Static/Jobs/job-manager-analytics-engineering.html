CURRENT OPENINGS AT FIREBRAND

Manager, Analytics Engineering

Location: Seattle, WA | Los Angeles, CA | New York, NY | SF Bay Area | Remote

About the Job


Firebrand is a pureplay analytics consultancy that delivers client impact via digital analytics, data platforms, and artificial intelligence solutions. Founded by analytics industry veterans, Firebrand values innovation, integrity, and impact. We are at the forefront of some of the largest AI transformations in the world. We pride ourselves on designing, building, and implementing innovative solutions which drive real value for our clients, helping them improve operational efficiencies, improve customer satisfaction, and create new revenue streams. As a new company, we are entrepreneurial and always looking to shape our business to meet the rapidly changing requirements of our customers.

The Manager, Analytics Engineering will work with data scientists and proprietary tools to provide data-informed insights and solutions to guide development and execution of customer strategies. The successful candidate is a big picture thinker that enjoys diving deep, a strong communicator (both written and verbal), an influencer and entrepreneur. This role sits at the intersection of data and strategy, and requires very strong right brain/left interaction with seasoned judgment.

The successful candidate will understand how to lead strategic thinking, scope business questions, build a learning agenda, and collaborate with data scientists to build out the data on which actionable insights can be delivered. This person needs to be an expert in big data and advanced statistics, and a critical thinker with the hunger to identify and solve business challenges through data. The role will lead experimentation and develop hypotheses around key strategies and insights, and manage ambiguity and competing priorities while staying focused on short-term and long-term goals.

The ideal candidate is highly collaborative, but self-driven with a strong bias for action and an ability to understand and deliver ambiguous projects with incomplete data. They are a strong technical thinker focused on business intelligence with strategy expertise and a working knowledge of or experience with multi-touch customer behavior analysis. This role requires a resourceful entrepreneur mentality that finds creative solutions to complex technical and business challenges.


Job Responsibilities


  • Ensure project scope, requirements and specifications are developed appropriately.
  • Manage client expectations around delivery and timelines.
  • Identify and assemble consulting resources quickly, ensuring knowledge hand-offs.
  • Ensure thorough and complete delivery of all committed functionality in a manner that meets or exceeds client expectations.
  • Gather and analyze statistical data using modern and traditional methods to formulate reports and make recommendations.
  • Assist in pioneering new approaches and work to best frame the use of data in all use cases from understanding churn and helping convert high value customers.
  • Devise and evaluate methods for collecting customer data.
  • Write scripts for insight generation automation (i.e., customer profile generation).
  • Collaborate with client teams to assess performance data and bring optimization solutions and recommendations.
  • Guide the Solution Architect in database querying and data mining with the understanding of the connections across data sets.
  • Develop trusted advisor relationships with the client and educate how people-based data can be leveraged across the planning, activation, and measurement cycle (aka growth hacking).
  • Remain fully informed on market trends, third party research, and implementation best practices.

Required Qualifications


  • 8+ years hands on or overseeing large, advanced analytics projects with big data platforms.
  • Strong analytical skills and ability to quickly transform the data into presentable formats that answer hard questions with current generation analytical reporting.
  • Experience working in or with large enterprises.
  • Prior consulting experience required.
  • Demonstrated skill in selecting the right statistical tools given a data analysis problem.
  • Experience articulating and translating business questions and using statistical techniques to arrive at an answer using available data.
  • Excellent communication skills (written and verbal) with the ability to explain complex concepts in a user-friendly manner.
  • Can-do attitude with the desire to develop professionally across the wider organization.
  • Ability to travel up to 25%.
  • Ability to work independently and set priorities for self and others.
  • BA/BS degree. Preference for mathematics, information systems, computer science, statistics, or related disciplines though equivalent experience may suffice.

Preferred Qualifications


  • Masters Degree in a quantitative discipline (CS, ML, Mathematics, Statistics).
  • Comfort and willingness to cultivate client relationships to understand key needs and develop new business with new and existing accounts.
  • Familiarity with building web service APIs to support data analytics algorithms.
  • Prior people management experience.
