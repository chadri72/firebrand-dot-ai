CURRENT OPENINGS AT FIREBRAND

Practice Director, Digital Analytics

Location: Seattle, WA | Hybrid

About the Job


Firebrand is a pureplay analytics consultancy that delivers client impact via digital analytics, data platforms, and artificial intelligence solutions. Founded by analytics industry veterans, Firebrand values innovation, integrity, and impact. We are at the forefront of some of the largest AI transformations in the world. We pride ourselves on designing, building, and implementing innovative solutions which drive real value for our clients, helping them improve operational efficiencies, improve customer satisfaction, and create new revenue streams. As a new company, we are entrepreneurial and always looking to shape our business to meet the rapidly changing requirements of our customers.

We are looking for a strong, dynamic leader to lead our Digital Analytics consulting practice based in Seattle, WA. This is a ground floor opportunity to build a practice with significant potential for growth and shared upside.

The Practice Director is responsible for growing and managing all aspects of the Digital Analytics practice, including designing & delivering high-quality solutions; identifying and closing new business opportunities; attracting, developing, & managing a strong talent base; and managing the practice financials. The qualified candidate will have 15+ years of dedicated Digital Analytics experience, be highly expert in designing and delivering digital analytics solutions for large enterprises, be expert at client interactions and expectation management, have experience leading diverse analytics teams, and have strong deep familiarity with prominent industry toolsets & vendors.

The responsibilities of the position are substantial, spanning Solution Delivery, Business Development, Talent Development & Management, and Business Management.


Leadership & Solution Delivery


  • Ensure project scope, requirements and specifications are developed appropriately.
  • Manage client expectations around delivery and timelines.
  • Identify and assemble consulting resources quickly, ensuring knowledge hand-offs.
  • Assist clients in evaluating and selecting appropriate tools and systems for their needs across data collection, tag management, reporting & analytics, and data visualization.
  • Ensure thorough and complete delivery of all committed functionality in a manner that meets or exceeds client expectations.
  • Collaborate with other practices to deliver cross-functional solutions for clients.

Business Development


  • Proactively cultivate client relationships to understand key needs, and develop new business with new and existing accounts.
  • Collaborate closely with Firebrand Business Development and other practice leaders to develop leads and shepherd them through the sales process.
  • Opportunistically cultivate new leads for other Firebrand practices.
  • Work with key Partners to drive mutual success across product + professional services implementations & operations.

Talent Development & Management


  • Lead and manage a team of up to 20 consulting professionals.
  • Attract, interview, and hire new talent while maintaining a high quality bar.
  • Ensure that Digital Analytics team members are appropriately trained.
  • Ensure effective people management throughout all levels of the practice.
  • Ensure that high performers have strong career development paths, and that low performance situations are addressed quickly and thoughtfully.

Business Management


  • Manage the Digital Analytics P&L, driving consistent top-line revenue growth and making appropriate expense and investment tradeoffs.
  • Manage key metrics, and provide appropriate communication to Finance and Executive staff about variance from key goals and metrics.
  • Participate in key executive meetings to ensure alignment and strong communication across practices and departments.
  • Manage consultant utilization across the entire Digital Analytics team.
  • Contribute to Firebrand’s thought leadership and content strategy.

Required Qualifications


  • 15+ years experience in Digital Analytics.
  • Proven track record leading, managing, and developing people.
  • Experience working in or with large enterprises.
  • Prior consulting experience required.
  • Ability to travel up to 25%.
  • Ability to work independently and set priorities for self and others.
  • BA/BS degree. Preference for mathematics, information systems, computer science, statistics, or related disciplines though equivalent experience may suffice.

Preferred Qualifications


  • Deep fluency with Adobe Analytics.
  • Experience with Adobe Target, Adobe Audience Manager, and Adobe CJA.
  • Javascript skills.
  • SQL skills.
  • Experience with tag management tools and techniques.
  • Prior experience with optimization tools such as Optimizely.
  • Project management skills.

Firebrand offers competitive pay and benefits, with additional incentives for high performing leaders.
