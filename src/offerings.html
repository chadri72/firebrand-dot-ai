How We Engage

Each client has unique needs, and we flex our engagement model to those needs. We can build a custom engagement that works for you and your unique circumstances

Our two most common engagement types are fixed-bid project work and managed services. Offerings like strategy and development work tend to have defined beginnings and ends, and are best suited for fixed-bid project engagement models. Conversely, ongoing support such as ML Ops or solution maintenance are better suited to a managed service model.
