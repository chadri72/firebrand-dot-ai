Generative AI for BI

Request A Demo

A truly bespoke solution that leverages the best LLM models and YOUR proprietary data, in a fully private environment.

Offering Overview

Firebrand's "AI4BI" solution leverages the latest LLM technology such as GPT4 and combines it with your proprietary BI data and knowledge bases to create a true conversational insight-generating interface. Instead of making ad hoc requests to an analytics team, your leaders will be able to converse directly with the LLM to gain the answers they need quickly. The solution is customized and branded for your company, provides both graphical and text-based output, and is contextually aware, meaning it remembers what it's been asked before by that individual and can keep context as it goes, just like in a regular conversation. The end result is rapid insight generation and speedier decisions.

Key Benefits

The AI4BI solution is geared toward rapid insight generation and is meant to be used by analysts, managers, and well-trained executives.

Benefits include:

  • Solution fully customized to your needs.
  • Conversational interface that outputs graphs and text.
  • "Stateful" context window that remembers what you've already asked it so you can converse in a natural way.
  • Multiple forms & sources of data available through a single interface, with the LLM performing the synthesis.
  • Hosted in your private cloud environment so that no data is shared back with the LLM.
  • Capable of answering complex questions -- it's like a Data Scientist in your pocket!

What's Under the Hood?

Firebrand's approach takes a General Intelligence LLM such as GPT4 and couples it with your proprietary data, creating what we call a "Hybrid LLM." The technical term for this approach is Retrieval Augmented Generation, or RAG, where the LLM is paired with a retrieval-based data store (a vector database in this case) to adapt a model to a given use case and custom data. This approach works quite well, and when built on a robust cloud service such as Azure, offers many further ways to expand and integrate its impact with other teams and systems. For example, the same general approach can be used to create a conversational interface for Sales people or Customer Support agents.

Beyond initial setup & training, the solution also captures the questions it's being asked and adds them to the Knowledge Base. Thus over time, the solution gets smarter as it gets used more. We call this phenomenon "Social Sharing", and believe it will be a source of substantial value for enterprises in the coming years.
