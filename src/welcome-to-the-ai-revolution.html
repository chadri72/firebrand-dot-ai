Welcome to the AI Revolution

The Computing Revolution. The Internet Revolution. The Mobile Revolution. All had a profound impact on the way we live and work. The AI Revolution offers us no less.

"AI is the defining technology of our times." – Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft

There have been several technological revolutions in the past few decades. The Computing revolution of the 80s and 90s changed how companies do business. The Internet and Mobile revolutions changed the everyday lives of most people on the planet. Parallel revolutions are occurring in transportation (e.g. electrification of cars) and healthcare (e.g. vaccines, telemedicine.)

But one revolution will tie these revolutions together, amplifying and compounding their effects: the AI revolution. Simply put, AI can see patterns in data that humans cannot. It can make predictions from those patterns, and incorporate its accuracy back into the next prediction. AI can learn deeper, broader, and faster than a human. And it's here right now.

Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google, recently said, "AI is the most important technology of our time, and it's going to have a profound impact on every industry." AI’s impacts will be very broad, especially for enterprises. AI can help enterprises improve revenue and reduce costs by delivering personalized and intuitive customer experiences, automating back-office functions, and helping managers make better decisions faster. In short, AI is a gamechanger.

But as with every other technology, AI has its pitfalls. AI is only as good as the data it consumes. It must be developed with safety in mind and monitored for unexpected behaviors. Basically, good AI requires good data and good governance. It requires an understanding of how the technology works and how best to apply it. Most companies are only at the very beginning of this learning curve.

Enter Firebrand. We have real-world AI experience. We have collectively delivered hundreds of Data Science and AI projects for large enterprises. We understand how to apply AI technologies to the right use cases, quickly and efficiently. We help enterprises create AI-powered customer experiences and business processes.

At Firebrand, we are helping lead the AI revolution. And we couldn't be more excited for its prospects.
