About

Leadership

Firebrand is led by experienced industry practitioners who helped pioneer analytics, big data, and data science in the Digital age. Our backgrounds include large tech companies, global consultancies, and top-tier agencies.

David Sogn

Data Science Leader

David specializes in Data Science for digital marketing and media management. Prior to Firebrand, David was Chief Data Officer for Initiative (an IPG company), Vice President of Data Science at HCL, Director of Analytics at Ogilvy, and analyst at Microsoft and Disney. David holds an MA from Cambridge.

Kevin Woo

Digital Intelligence Leader

Kevin specializes in digital marketing and digital intelligence. Prior to Firebrand, Kevin was co-founder and President of digital consultancy Pointmarc (acquired by Merkle in 2015), SVP at Merkle, Sr Marketing Analyst at Microsoft, and client strategist at Razorfish and Saatchi. Kevin holds a BA from Princeton.

Chad Richeson

AI Strategy Leader

Chad specializes in AI strategy and big data systems for large enterprises. Prior to Firebrand, Chad was CEO of Society Consulting (acquired by Ernst & Young in 2016), Partner in EY's Global AI & Analytics practice, General Manager at Amazon, and Director of Big Data at Microsoft. Chad holds an MBA from Tulane University.

Augustus Saunders

AI Practice Leader

One of the select few to deploy multiple customer-facing LLMs at scale, "Gus" has a long history of building compelling AI solutions for Fortune 500 companies including Nestle, FedEx, Merck and Amazon, parlaying his software development background into complex data architectures and data-driven solutions.

Shane Atchison

Advisor

A true digital pioneer, Shane provides market insight and guidance to the Firebrand founding team. Shane was previously CEO of NetRush, CMO of Domo, CEO of Wunderman, and CEO of Possible.

Our Culture

Firebrand culture is a vibrant blend of fun, quirkiness, and unwavering drive. We believe that a workplace should be more than just a daily routine; it should be a place where creativity flourishes and employees can be their genuine selves.

Beneath the surface of our playful atmosphere lies a deep commitment to excellence and continuous improvement. We love to receive feedback and do not hesitate to give it. We believe when people feel comfortable being themselves, they are more likely to share ideas fearlessly and push boundaries. Our work environment fosters this spirit of creativity and encourages employees to take calculated risks in pursuit of innovation.
