Data Strategy for a Global Apparel Company

We built a comprehensive data strategy for one of the world's largest apparel companies. This strategy spanned technology, people, and process for all geographies and sales channels, including first party online and physical stores.

The Challenge

Our client, a global apparel company, needed to modernize its data infrastructure to help it manage its multiple brands, thousands of SKUs, and complex channel network. Technologies needed to be updated, taxonomies and processes needed to be brought together, and various analytical teams needed to work from a single source of truth. Complicating matters was the fact that third party resellers were inconsistent or incomplete with providing data back to the company, causing further issues.

The Solution

We worked closely across various client teams to design a comprehensive strategy to document and prioritize core capabilities and matrix it with key technologies, taxonomies, and applications. The resulting decisions were delivered via roadmaps with key capabilities documented in detail. We implemented a review framework to evaluate progress, correct errors, and keep all relevant parties informed. We also devised a new incentive plan to increase the compliance of third party resellers by sharing aggregated intelligence with those in compliance.

The Results


The data strategy was completed in 8 weeks, rolled out to dozens of affected teams, and integrated into the annual planning process of the client going forward.
