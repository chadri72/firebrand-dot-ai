Data Science for Fraud Prevention

We worked with a large technology company to create a distributed data platform for a large team of Fraud Prevention Data Scientists. The platform contained over 2,000 data features, with room for growth, and was used by over 20 Data Scientists.

The Challenge

Our client, a large consumer technology company, ran a payments system that processed tens of millions of transactions daily. A small portion of these transactions were committed by fraudulent actors and needed to be identified and prevented via the use of machine learning models. The data science team in charge of fraud prevention needed a centralized big data platform to support and coordinate their modeling and feature development activities.

The Solution

Our team worked closely with the client to design a new data platform within the client’s current technology stack. We leveraged a distributed processing technology similar to Hadoop to handle the large data sets, and connected these data sets to a pipelining and query capability with a SQL-like front end. We also built a feature store that enabled the data scientists to catalog and organize the 2,000+ data features they had discovered through their analysis, with room to expand.

The Results


The solution was completed in 6 months and went immediately into production, supporting a team of 20+ data scientists and impacting millions of customers.
